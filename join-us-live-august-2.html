Join Us Live August 2: A Free Session on Building Courses That Transform

We're going live to show you exactly what outcome-driven course design looks like in practice — and give you a framework you can apply to your own content immediately.

By Engaging Courses  ·  July 2025

Event details

Date Sunday, August 2, 2026
Time 11:00am ET
Cost Free
Format Live online — link sent to registered attendees

We've spent a lot of time writing about the difference between courses that inform and courses that transform. On August 2, we're going to show you.

This free live session is designed for course creators at any stage — whether you're building your first course or you've been at it for years and wondering why your results aren't matching your effort.

What we'll cover

  • Why most online courses fail to engage learners — and the one design shift that changes everything
  • What outcome-driven course design actually looks like in practice, with live examples from our own courseware
  • How illustrated storytelling and animation can be used as teaching tools — not just decoration
  • A framework for auditing your existing course content for the most common engagement gaps
  • A live look at Engaging Courses Academy and what's inside

Who this is for

This session is for you if you've created online course content — or you're thinking about it — and you want to understand why some courses hold attention and drive results while others don't.

It's for subject-matter experts who know their content inside out but aren't sure how to translate that expertise into a learning experience that actually works. It's for experienced course creators who are getting completions but not the reviews and referrals they expected. And it's for anyone curious about a different approach to online education — one that puts learner transformation at the center of everything.

What makes this different

We're not going to spend this session telling you to buy something. We're going to teach. You'll leave with ideas you can apply immediately, regardless of whether you ever join Engaging Courses Academy.

That's our philosophy as course creators: demonstrate your value first. Give people something real. Let the quality of the teaching speak for itself.

It's also, not coincidentally, exactly what we teach our students to do.
